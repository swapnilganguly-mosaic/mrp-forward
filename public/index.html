Bulk paste demand

Paste three columns: sku_code, Month-1 units, Month-2 units (tab, comma, or space separated — one SKU per line). Two columns (sku, M1) also work and leave M2 unchanged. Matching is case-insensitive on the SKU code; existing demand for matched SKUs is overwritten.

Import demand from a file

Upload one spreadsheet listing each SKU and its Month-1 and Month-2 demand. The recipe (RM % & PM quantities per unit) is already built in — only the demand columns are read from this file. Accepted: .xlsx or .csv (not the older .xls). SKU codes are matched case-insensitively.
